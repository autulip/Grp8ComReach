About Kapwa Ko Mahal Ko

In Kapwa Ko Mahal Ko, it is our duty to bring a smile on children who are less fortunate.

Our mission is to create a meaningful impact by bringing joy, fostering awareness, and offering enriching educational opportunities that benefit not only the children we aim to support but also ourselves. Through this initiative, we hope to develop a shared journey of growth, learning, and compassion.
